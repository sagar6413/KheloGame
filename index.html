TIC TAC TOE

Tic-tac-toe, noughts and crosses, or Xs and Os is a paper-and-pencil game for two players who take turns marking the spaces in a three-by-three grid with X or O. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row is the winner.

7 UP - 7 DOWN

7 Up 7 Down is a dice game that is played by betting on numbers more than 7 or less than 7. Two dices are rolled in the game and if the addition of numbers on both the dices matches your bet then the player wins.
